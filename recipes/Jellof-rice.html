Jellof Rice Recipe

Jellof rice

Jellof rice is a popular nigerian cusine thats a favorite in parties and celebrations.

This is an easy recipe hack to have your yummy jellof rice ready in 35mins, your jellof rice can be enjoyed together with salad,plantain,chicken etc.

Ingridents
  1. 2-½ cups Parboiled long grain rice (Washed thoroughly and drained)
  2. 16 oz can tomato paste
  3. 4 cups chicken stock or water (hot but not scalding)
  4. ½ cup cooking oil of choice
  5. ½ tsp curry powder
  6. tsp dry thyme
  7. 1 tsp garlic powder
  8. 1 tsp ground ginger
  9. 1 small green pepper(chopped)
